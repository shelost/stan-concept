How to Upgrade/ Downgrade Your Stan Subscription

View in Help Scout

Upgrading from Creator to Creator PRO

1. Login to your Stan account and click Settings in the bottom left corner of your screenClick to Zoom

2. Next, scroll down until you see the section labeled Your Stan Subscription. Click on the button that says Change Plan.

3. Select between Monthly or Yearly, then select the Creator PRO plan and click Upgrade Now.

Upgrading your plan will prorate & charge your card immediately

If you are upgrading from a monthly to yearly plan, or upgrading from Creator to Creator PRO, we will prorate your current plan with your billing cycle and charge the remainder of the new plan immediately.

Downgrading from Creator PRO to Creator

1. Login to your Stan account and click Settings in the bottom left corner of your screen

2. Next, scroll down until you see the section labeled Your Stan Subscription. Click on the button that says Change Plan.

3. Select between Monthly or Yearly, then select the Creator plan and click Downgrade Now.

Downgrading will not go into affect until the end of your billing cycle

If you are downgrading from a Yearly to Monthly plan or from Creator PRO to Creator, your plan change will be scheduled and will not go into affect until the end of your current billing cycle.

i.e. If you have signed up for the Creator PRO annual plan ($948/year) and you would like to change to Creator PRO monthly plan, your plan will not downgrade until the end of your 1 year billing cycle. After the year has passed, your plan will switch to Creator PRO monthly plan.  

If you have any questions, please reach out to friends@stanwith.me and we'd be happy to help!
